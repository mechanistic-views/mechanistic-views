# Steinhardt "The Case for Evaluating Model Behaviors"

- **URL**: https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/J5KkwYnnaeNX7hL2s/the-case-for-evaluating-model-behaviors
- **Year**: 2026
- **Author**: Jacob Steinhardt
- **Problems**: ~37 research directions
- **Focus**: Behavioral propensity measurement, capability vs propensity distinction

## Key Claim
The field over-invests in measuring what models *can* do (capability) and under-invests in what they *tend* to do (propensity). Maps to Instrumental vs Role view distinction.
