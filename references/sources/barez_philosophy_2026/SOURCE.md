# Barez et al. "Mechanistic Interpretability Needs Philosophy"

- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.18852
- **PDF**: paper.pdf (local)
- **Year**: 2026
- **Focus**: Conceptual foundations and epistemic status of MI

## Key Claim
MI needs philosophy for clarifying concepts, refining methods, and navigating epistemic and ethical complexities.
Uses three open problems from MI literature as examples.
