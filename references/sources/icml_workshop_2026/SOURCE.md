# ICML 2026 Mechanistic Interpretability Workshop CFP

- **URL**: https://mechinterpworkshop.com/cfp/
- **Year**: 2026
- **Problems**: ~30 open questions across 6 themes
- **Focus**: Field-level open questions for workshop submissions

## Themes
1. High-level properties and internal use of representations
2. Cognitive phenomena (latent reasoning, planning, world models)
3. Discovery methods (circuits, causal, attribution, dictionary learning)
4. Downstream applications and debugging
5. Benchmarking progress
6. Safety applications (alignment faking, emergent misalignment)
7. Scaling and generalization
8. Automation of interpretability
9. Framework and definitions ("What is the right framework?", "How should features/circuits be defined?")
